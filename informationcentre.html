Information Centres

In this modern world books are been dominated by electronic media. Even students prefer pdf s over pages. In this way the information can be stored in a safe manner for many years. Information centres is an innovative idea which helps students in every way by providing help 24/7. Such centres will be in every building of our VIT.

Facilities available:

Notifications from Registrar

Downloading or Uploading of material

Professors timetable and contact info

Clarification of doubts regarding academics and non-academics

Setup of Information Centres:

The libraries available in every building will be modified into these advanced information centres with well-configured computers. The notifications from the registrar will be automatically downloaded from academics site and will be displayed in a led screen (40 inch). There will be a Wi-Fi hotspot (ftp) in which all the materials available in the "course-page" can be downloaded. There will also another system in which students can upload the material which they find more helpful, but not available in course-page, with specific authorization.

There will be also a group of three to five who can clarify any doubts regarding academics like which subject to be taken in which semester and so on.

The students can also express the need for the change in syllabus, if any changes required.

Budget Required for Setup Of Information Centres:

As mentioned earlier the Information centre will be in each building. Either by converting school libraries into Information centres or by providing a special room for it. The estimated price for each information centre will be around Rs.1.5 lakh.
