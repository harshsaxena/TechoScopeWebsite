About The Project

The project aims to bring to notice the technological developments in various fields which can be implemented, maintained and also used to improve the existing systems in VIT University.

The reason that we chose a website to convey our ideas is the fact that, internet nowadays is one of the best mediums to reach a large audience and spread ideas. We live in a digital world today and therefore there can be no better way than a website, which can bring to notice the various technological developments which can bring the university at par with the leading universities of the world.

We have developed this website keeping in view,primarily the students and professors of VIT University, who might be interested in implementing or improving the various technical aspects mentioned on the website.

The resources brought to the project mainly involve web page creation, knowledge of HTML and CSS and information and research gathered from the internet. The basic concepts and technologies which we have mentioned, are the ones we learnt about and came across during the development of the website.
